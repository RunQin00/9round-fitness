Our Workouts

9ROUND DELIVERS THE ULTIMATE BODY TRANSFORMATION PROGRAM FOR ALL FITNESS LEVELS

What to Expect:

  • Show up at your convenience, there are no class times
  • Friendly trainer teaches you how to wrap your hands for support when punching
  • Use heart rate training to stay in your target fat burning zone
  • Every three minutes, you move to the next exercise station
  • After station nine, your 30 minute full body workout is complete, and you can go for a second round if you would like to !


    • What you'll accomplish

  • Burn massive amounts of calories during your workout and continue burning for hours after workout is complete
  • Boost your stamina and endurance
  • Release stress, increase energy, improve sleep and much more!

    • Stations 1-2: Strength

    Here, you will use dumb bells, kettle bells, medicine balls, jump ropes and other equipment to build strength and stamina.



    Stations 3-8: Kickboxing Fitness

    The real fun begins as you punch and kick heavy bags to build power, double end bags to increase coordination and timing, and speed bags to develop shoulder conditioning and speed.



    Station 9: Abs and Core

    You will tighten and strengthen all the muscles in one of your most common trouble spots, your core, which improves your ability to perform andy movement you for work, exercise or leisure.
